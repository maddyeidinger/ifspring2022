Maddy's Miso Soup Recipe

I love soup. And I like cooking a moderate amount. But after a long day of classed, I’m tried and I’m lazy and I'm hungry. And all I want is a hot bowl of warm soup ASAP. So I started making miso soup. It has become a staple in my routine and my life. Its verstile, its healthy, and its delicious! Here is my adapted Miso Soup recipe!

The Recipe

Ingridents:

  • 4 cups of water (or vegtable stock or dashi)
  • 1-2 Sheets of Nori depending on your prefence
  • 3-4 TBSP of Miso Paste
  • 1 Cup of Chopped Tofu
  • 1 Cup of Chopped Green Onion
  • A splash of soy sauce

Instructions:

  1. Boil your water in a big pot, with a s
  2. When your water begins to boil, soup a cup of it into a bowl with your miso paste, and wisk to combine
  3. Pour the miso liquid into the water and turn the pot on low
  4. Wisk to combine
  5. Add in your tofu, green onion, soy sauce, nori
  6. Pour into a bowl and enjoy!

Adaptations:

  • There are a lot of fun ways to adapt the recipe and Im going to expand on that over here
  • wontons
  • egg
  • soba noodles
  • spinach
